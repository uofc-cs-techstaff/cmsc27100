But then $\exists x \neg P(x)$ and $\neg \exists x \neg P(x)$ are both true—a contradiction. Therefore, our assumption was incorrect, and $P(u)$ is true. Therefore, our assumption was incorrect, and $\exists \neg P(x)$ is true.
