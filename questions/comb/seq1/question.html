Everyone loves the hot new internet word game Wordle. In Wordle, the player guesses a five letter word, and the tiles will change colour:

  • If the tile is green, then the letter is in the answer and in the correct spot
  • If the tile is yellow, then the letter is in the answer but in the wrong spot
  • If the tile is grey, then the letter is not in the answer in any spot

However, we will be considering a more fun and easy version made just for combinatorialists. Instead of restricting answers to five letter words, we allow any five character string, made up of letters A through Z.
